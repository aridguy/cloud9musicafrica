Cloud9 Music Africa is one of the new Africa’s leading entertainment companies. Founded in 2018 as a record label by Akpujiha Fegor, Cloud9 Music Africa produced some of the definitive music and next rated African artistes such as , JayTrigga, Chris Da King, Lil Johnero, Madd Gorilla, King$oul and KD the Lit Guy.

Currently, its roster spans a variety of genres with African artists locally and in the diaspora.

As the digital era continues to refashion the way we do things, Cloud9 Music Africa taste for delivering the utmost value has inspired the company’s expansion into innovative services such as Festival partnerships, Content production, Music Publishing and Marketing.

Today, Cloud9 Music Africa is a community of amazing people dedicated to creativity, innovation and breaking new frontiers.
